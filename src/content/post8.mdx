This programme prepares candidates for the PMI® certification exam including:

1. Pre-assessment of eligibility of all candidates prior to course confirmation
2. Provision of CV templates, assistance with completion of CVs, quality check, online CV application to PMI®
3. Certification Orientation Session
4. Project Management Alignment Online Course
5. 35 hours of face to face training covering the 10 Knowledge Areas, Professional Responsibility and Exam Preparation
6. Sample Tests
7. Assistance with all communication with PMI® in the USA
